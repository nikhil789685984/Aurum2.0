Our Philosophy

Where every plate
tells a story

AURUM is a celebration of Indian flavours in a modern setting. We use seasonal produce and traditional techniques to create food that feels premium, familiar, and memorable.

For us, dining is an experience, not just a meal. From the welcome drink to the final dessert, every detail is crafted with care, balance, and hospitality.

Our ingredients are sourced from trusted farms, spice growers, and artisan producers across India, bringing authentic regional taste to every plate.

A Vision
Uncompromising

Born into a family that celebrated home-cooked food, Nikhil developed his palate early through regional Indian kitchens and local street food culture.

He opened AURUM in 2009 with one clear idea: present Indian dishes with premium quality, modern plating, and authentic flavour that still feels rooted in tradition.

"The finest ingredient you can bring to any dish is your full attention."

Today Nikhil leads a team of passionate chefs, works directly with local producers across India, and mentors young culinary talent through in-house training programs.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01

Provenance Above All

We know the name of every farmer, fisherman, and forager who supplies our kitchen. Provenance is not a talking point — it is a commitment, verified in person on every visit.

02

Seasonality Without Compromise

We do not fly in ingredients out of season. Our menus change weekly, following the market and the weather. This constraint is the source of our creativity.

03

Zero Waste Kitchen

Since 2019, AURUM has diverted 98% of kitchen waste from landfill. Ferments, stocks, and vinegars from trim and peelings. Composting with our farm suppliers. A circular kitchen in practice.

04

People & Culture

We offer every member of our team a four-day working week, above-industry wages, and a clear development pathway. A kitchen where people thrive produces better food.
