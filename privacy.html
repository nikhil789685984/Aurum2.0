Privacy Policy

AURUM collects the information you submit through account creation, reservation requests, and delivery orders in order to operate the service, contact you about your request, and fulfill payments and bookings.

What We Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, and reservation details.
  • Delivery address details for food orders.
  • Payment references returned by payment providers. We do not store full card details.

How We Use Data

  • To manage reservations and customer support.
  • To process delivery orders and payment confirmations.
  • To send service emails such as OTPs, confirmations, and order notices.
